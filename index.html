About us

Let's Sea is an exclusive exporter of seafood from India. We have unmatched expertise in hatchery and farming.

From a very small unit of hatcheries operations in a leased processing facility in Bhimavaram and Yanam, Let's Sea successfully ventured into the seafood industry through backward integration into deep-sea trawling and seafood processing.

Let's Sea has been exporting substantial quantities of freshwater shrimps in various product forms both raw and processed.
